About Us

Milton's home for premium barbering. Golden Chair Barbershop is where skilled hands and quality craft come together. No appointments, no waiting lists. Just walk in, take a seat and leave looking your best.

From men's haircuts and fades to kids' cuts, beard trims, hot towel shaves and full head shaves. We do it all, right here at 480 Bronte St S Unit 113-B, Milton.
